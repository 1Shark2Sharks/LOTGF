Mrs. Orianna is an accountant of the [[Blackthorn Security Company]] in [[Tingen City]].
Sixteen years ago, she was studying accountancy in business school. Back then the serial killer, the Bloody Butcher, was appeared in the city, five girls killed consecutively. Orianna almost became the sixth victim, fortunately, the [[Nighthawks]] had come in time. After that incident, she works as an accountant of the Blackthorn Security Company ever since.

### Appearance

She is a black-haired woman dressed in a flounce lace dress. She appeared in her thirties and has fashionable curly hair. Her pair of green eyes were clear and smiling, and she seems  refined and elegant.
